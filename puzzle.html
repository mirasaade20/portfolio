Demo Video

Project Overview

The Problem

I wanted to visualize and compare AI search algorithms on a real, interactive puzzle — not just run them in the terminal.

My Approach

Built a full multi-screen app in Python using Pygame. The app has 5 screens: Home, Solo Play, AI Race, Statistics, and Settings.

Result

A fully playable and resizable puzzle app where you can play manually or watch two AI heuristics race to solve the same board simultaneously.

Course

Built for AI course (CSC 310) at the American University of Technology.

Features

  • 🧩 Solo Play — play manually with an A* hint button on demand
  • 🤖 AI Race — watch Manhattan Distance vs Linear Conflict heuristics race side by side
  • 📊 Statistics Dashboard — benchmark multiple puzzles and view performance charts
  • 🎨 Color Themes — multiple visual themes selectable from Settings
  • 📐 Resizable Window — scales perfectly to any screen size, fullscreen with F11
