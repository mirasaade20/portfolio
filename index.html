8-Puzzle Lab

A sliding puzzle game with an A* AI solver, side-by-side heuristic race, and a statistics dashboard — built in Python.

Python Pygame AI / A* Algorithm
The Problem

Wanted to visualize and compare AI search algorithms on a real interactive puzzle.

My Approach

Built 5 screens: Home, Solo Play (with A* hint on demand), AI Race (Manhattan vs Linear Conflict side-by-side), Statistics dashboard, and Settings with color themes and puzzle size options.

Result

A fully playable and resizable puzzle app where you can play manually or watch two AI heuristics race to solve the same board simultaneously.
